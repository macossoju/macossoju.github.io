WHO WE ARE

Welcome to Pride Models Africa!

We are a premier African model agency dedicated to showcasing the beauty, diversity, and talent of African models to the world. Our agency represents a diverse range of models from across the African continent, including various ethnicities, body types, and ages.

At Pride Models, we are passionate about promoting African fashion, culture, and creativity. We work closely with top designers, brands, and industry professionals to provide our models with exceptional opportunities to shine on both local and international runways, fashion campaigns, and editorial features.

Our mission is to empower and uplift African models, helping them achieve their dreams and break barriers in the fashion industry. We believe in fostering a supportive and inclusive environment that values authenticity, individuality, and professionalism.

With our experienced team and extensive industry connections, we offer personalized guidance, training, and career development to our models, ensuring they are well-prepared and equipped to succeed in the competitive world of modeling.

Join us at Pride Models as we redefine beauty standards and champion the extraordinary talents that Africa has to offer. We are committed to making a positive impact and creating a platform that celebrates African beauty, diversity, and excellence.
